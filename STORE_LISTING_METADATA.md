## Full Description

**WOPP** is the official mobile app for **Men and Women of Passion and Purpose** — a vibrant faith-based community built to connect, equip, and empower believers to live with passion and purpose every day.

Stay connected to your ministry wherever you are. WOPP brings announcements, events, programs, mentorship, and digital resources together in one beautifully designed experience.

### What you can do with WOPP

**Stay Informed**
- Receive timely announcements and ministry updates
- Get push notifications for important community news
- Tap notifications to go directly to the content that matters

**Events & Programs**
- Browse upcoming events and register with ease
- Discover empowerment programs and track your enrollment
- View event and program details, schedules, and locations

**Mentorship & Growth**
- Explore mentorship classes and sessions
- Follow structured paths for spiritual and personal development
- Access mentorship details and share opportunities with others

**Digital Library**
- Browse ebooks and faith-based resources
- Build your personal library of saved content
- Read PDF resources in-app with a smooth reading experience

**Clips & Media**
- Watch ministry clips and short-form video content
- Explore featured messages and teaching highlights

**Membership & Subscriptions**
- Manage your membership status
- Access premium content and subscription benefits
- Stay informed about grace periods and renewal options

**Community Policies**
- Review terms of use, privacy policy, and community guidelines
- Understand content sharing rules before participating

**Built for Community**
WOPP is designed for members, leaders, and administrators who want a secure, modern platform for ministry engagement — not just another social app, but a purpose-driven community hub.

Join **Men and Women of Passion and Purpose** on WOPP and stay connected to what God is doing in your community.
